Refund & Return Policy

Business Name: Foodly

Contact Email: servicefoodly@gmail.com

At Foodly, we strive to provide high-quality food and excellent customer service. This Refund & Return Policy outlines the terms and conditions for refund eligibility.

1. Refund Eligibility

Refunds will only be considered under the following circumstances:

  • Incorrect Order: If the food items provided are different from the items ordered.
  • Quality Issues: If the food is spoiled, contaminated, or unfit for consumption upon pickup or during dine-in.
  • Missed Orders: If an order was placed, paid for, and not prepared.

2. Non-Refundable Situations

  • Change of mind after the food has been prepared.
  • Delays caused by the customer in picking up their order.
  • Complaints about food quality after leaving the outlet or more than 30 minutes after pickup.
  • Frequent refund requests from the same customer without valid reasoning.

3. Refund Request Process

  • Contact Us: Email servicefoodly@gmail.com within 24 hours of receiving your order.
  • Provide Proof: Include your order ID, a brief description of the issue, and supporting evidence (e.g., a photo of the food).

4. Refund Approval & Processing

  • If approved, refunds will be credited to your registered bank account within 7-14 working days.
  • If paid using loyalty points, the points will be recredited within 24 hours of approval.
  • Refunds may also be issued as app credits or vouchers at Foodlyâ€™s discretion.

5. Prevention of Abuse

  • Frequent refund requests without valid reasons may be flagged for review.
  • Foodly reserves the right to deny further refund requests or suspend accounts in case of abuse.

6. Dine-In & Pickup Rules

  • For dine-in orders, concerns about food quality must be raised immediately while at the outlet.
  • For pickup orders, customers are encouraged to check their order before leaving the outlet.

7. No Return Policy & No Shipping Service

  • Once food has been picked up or consumed, it cannot be returned.
  • Foodly does not provide shipping or delivery services. Orders are for dine-in or pickup only.

8. Policy Updates

  • Foodly reserves the right to update this refund policy at any time.
  • Any changes will be communicated via email or app notifications.

For any questions or concerns, please contact us at servicefoodly@gmail.com.
